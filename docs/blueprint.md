# **App Name**: Oello Shop

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase (Home): Display high-quality images and key benefits of Breylee Teeth Whitening Powder. Details from grandstores.com.ng.
- Sales Funnel Integration: Implement a landing page designed to convert visitors into customers through strategic placement of information and CTAs.
- Order Form (Order Page): A form to collect customer details for pay-on-delivery orders, mirroring grandstores.com.ng/breylee-whitening-powder-order/.
- Thank You Page: Display confirmation message post-form submission with a prominent 'Go to Home' button.
- Customer Review Display: Showcase testimonials and reviews to build trust and social proof.
- FAQ Section: Address common questions and concerns regarding the product.
- Mobile Responsiveness: Ensure seamless experience across various devices (desktops, tablets, smartphones)

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#F4B942) for highlighting premium features and call to actions, drawing from the original product design.
- Background color: Off-White (#F8F9FA) to provide a clean and modern backdrop, similar to CloudPillow's aesthetic, allowing the gold accents to stand out.
- Accent color: Deep Blue (#1A2F54) to complement the gold and create a sense of trust and sophistication, used sparingly to highlight key information.
- Headline font: 'Playfair Display', serif, for elegant headlines, emphasizing key selling points.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, providing clarity and readability for product descriptions, testimonials, and FAQs.
- Use simple, clear icons in 'gold' to highlight product benefits and features. Style should match a balance of 'outline' and 'filled' style as needed. All icons will follow guidelines, existing in a format of 'SVG preferred'.
- Implement a single-page layout with clear sections for product information, benefits, customer reviews, and the order form, encouraging users to quickly move through the sales funnel. Utilize a maximum width for central sections to encourage greater attention.
